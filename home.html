About Me

I'm a web developer and an average programmer living in Vietnam. I mainly focus on front-end development but can also work with back-end development, like my website, blog site, API, or programs that run on browsers.

That said, I also have experience in game-making (Unity, Godot), GUI apps (Tauri, Electron, C#), and mobile apps (Flutter). However, it's not my main focus right now.

Since I believe in freedom, all my projects are Free and Open-Source, so you can contribute to the repository, use it somewhere else, or do whatever you want with it.
